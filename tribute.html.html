Mahatma Gandhi

The Father Of Nation

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi

  • 1869 - Birth at Porbandar.
  • 1888 - Reached Southampton, England "
  • 1888 - Enrolled at the Inner Temple, Inn of Court"
  • 1893 - Thrown off the first class carriage of the train at Pietermartizburg Station.
  • 1894 -Founded the 'Natal Indian Congress'.
  • 1896 -Commenced writing the 'Green Pamphlet' at Rajkot.
  • 1942 - Receives a Ph.D. in Genetics and Plant Pathology
  • 1906 - Advent of Satyagraha at Empire Theatre, Johannesburg, where people took a pledge with God as their witness, to oppose the Black Act.
  • 1909 - 'Hind Swarajya' published in 'Indian Opinion'.
  • 1919 - National Hartal and Satyagraha against the Rowlett Act.
  • 1928 - Boycott of Simon Commission.
  • 1931 - Attended (in his loin-cloth) the Royal reception for the delegates of the Second Round Table Conference at the Buckingham Palace at the invitation of Emperor George V.
  • 1947 - The Congress, the Muslim League and the representatives of the Sikhs accept the plan of Partition.
